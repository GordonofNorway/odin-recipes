Salted pig parts

You will need 300g of Pork belly, 500ml of water, 1cm of ginger, and 1 scallion per serving.

  1. Cut pork fat into even cubes. Separate meat from fat.
  2. Boil a pot of water to rolling boil, add ginger and scallion. Then add cubed pork. Once water boils again, remove pork and rinse.
  3. Heat wok to medium. Add cubed pork. Once oil is produced, reduce heat to low. Cook until color turns golden brown
  4. Add pinch of salt and enjoy
